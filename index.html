About Greg

Greg Kitchen builds practical AI systems that make complex signals easier to understand and act on. His work sits at the intersection of strategy, market sensing, product thinking, and rapid prototyping.

The through line is translation: turning ambiguity into structure, structure into tools, and tools into clearer decisions for people doing consequential work.
